General Instructions:

Minimum contrast required:

Use a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text
Use at least 3:1 for large text (18pt or 14pt bold)


Simple rule:

Dark text on light background, OR
Light text on dark background
NEVER light on light, or dark on dark


Verification:

Test readability before implementing
Use contrast checking tools (WebAIM, Contrast Checker)
